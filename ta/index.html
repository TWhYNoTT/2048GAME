About The event

The Statistics Centre - Abu Dhabi (SCAD) organises Abu Dhabi Statistics Stakeholders Forum to enhance collaboration between government entities and the private sector in developing Abu Dhabi’s statistical ecosystem. The Forum focuses on the topic of digital transformation within the realm of statistics and the development of Abu Dhabi’s decentralised statistical ecosystem. It also discusses enhancing the statistical capabilities of government entities with the aim of achieving statistical maturity. Additionally, the Forum emphasises the importance of data collaboration to enhance decision-making processes based on reliable and high-quality statistical indicators. At the forum, SCAD will pay tribute to its strategic stakeholders and field researchers, while also providing an overview of SCAD’s significant accomplishments through its collaborations with strategic stakeholders.
